il Sereno

Torno · Lago di Como · 4 Nights

The lake's most modern luxury stay — sleek architecture, floor-to-ceiling glass opening straight to the water, and interiors for design lovers.

My take: Two ways in here. The Garden Lake View Suite is the larger room (~860 sqft) and is 15% off right now — it does see the lake, just not head-on. The Grand Suite Lago trades a little size for a private terrace and a direct, front-and-center lake view. If the view is everything, go Grand Suite; if space and value matter more, the Garden suite is hard to beat.
