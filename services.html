Frequently Asked Questions

What types of trips do you specialize in?
I specialize in thoughtfully curated European itineraries, honeymoons, family and multi-generational travel, milestone celebrations, and luxury escapes to destinations like Napa Valley, Mexico, and Europe.
Do you charge for hotel bookings?
No. Hotel recommendations and bookings are complimentary and may include preferred partner amenities and exclusive perks when available.
Why book through Suites With Keith instead of AMEX Fine Hotels + Resorts or Chase?
The perks are similar. The experience isn't. Through my relationships with many luxury hotels, I help ensure your stay is personalized from before you arrive until you check out.
What if I already have travel booked?
In many cases, I can take over your reservation as your advisor, advocate on your behalf, and explore any available preferred partner amenities or additional benefits for your stay.
How far in advance should I inquire?
Whenever possible, I recommend reaching out three to six months before departure. That said, I can often accommodate shorter timelines.
What if I have AMEX/Chase/etc. points I'd like to use?
I provide a consulting service for AMEX/Chase/Citi/Bilt points so that you get the best value for your points, including working with you to help book international business/first class airfare. Please reach out to learn more.
Do you handle transfers?
Yes — seamless, door-to-door transfers are part of every itinerary I plan. Through my partnership with Hoper and other trusted partners, I arrange private helicopter charters, private boat transfers between islands like Sifnos, Paros, and Milos, luxury airport transfers, and premium chauffeured car service across Greece, Italy, France, and beyond. From a scenic seaplane or speedboat island-hop to a private car waiting at arrivals, I coordinate every connection so your journey runs effortlessly.
Do you handle excursions?
Absolutely. From a private boat day on Lake Como to a curated, high-touch shopping day in Paris, I design bespoke excursions and private experiences around exactly how you love to travel — private guides and drivers, culinary experiences and chef's tables, after-hours museum tours, wine tastings, and more. If you can imagine it, I'll build it into your trip.
Do you book private homes and villas?
Yes. Through my trusted partners, I have access to a hand-selected network of exceptional private homes and luxury villas all over the world, each vetted for quality and service. Because they come through my partner network, these stays include added benefits and, in most cases, no end-of-stay cleaning fee. Tell me the destination and the vibe, and I'll match you with the right home.
