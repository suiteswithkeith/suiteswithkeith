Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

At Suites With Keith, your privacy is important. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your personal information.

Information We Collect

When you submit an inquiry, request a travel consultation, subscribe to our newsletter, or contact us, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • Travel preferences and trip details
  • Destination interests
  • Budget and travel dates
  • Any additional information you choose to provide

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Plan and book travel experiences
  • Provide personalized recommendations
  • Send travel inspiration, updates, and newsletters (if you’ve opted in)
  • Improve our services and website

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information.

When necessary to plan or book your travel, we may share relevant information with trusted travel partners such as hotels, resorts, cruise lines, tour operators, destination management companies, and our host travel agency. Information is only shared as needed to provide requested services.

We may also share information if required by law.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. While no online transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure, we strive to use appropriate safeguards.

Marketing Communications

If you subscribe to receive updates, you may receive occasional emails featuring travel inspiration, destination guides, exclusive offers, or planning tips. You can unsubscribe at any time by using the unsubscribe link included in our emails.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to improve your browsing experience, understand website traffic, and enhance site functionality.

Your Choices

You may request to:

  • Access the personal information we have about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing communications

To make a request, please contact us using the information below.
